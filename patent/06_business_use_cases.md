# Business Use Cases

## Cross-domain enterprise value

The framework should be domain-adaptive. It can support insurance, banking, healthcare, retail, utilities, travel, telecom, and other domains by changing the input schema, rules, and contract.

## EXL-relevant business problems

- Lack of safe production-like data for client projects.
- Privacy restrictions on using real customer data.
- Difficulty creating large test datasets for migrations and QA.
- Poor demo data for sales and client workshops.
- Need for synthetic data in AI model development and validation.
- Need to simulate edge cases such as fraud, claims escalation, payment failure, delinquency, or abnormal behavior.
- Need for audit-ready evidence that generated data follows rules.

## Example domains

### Insurance

- Customer, policy, claim, payment, fraud, reserve, settlement, adjuster workflows.
- Validate claim lifecycle order and fraud thresholds.

### Banking

- Customer onboarding, KYC, account opening, transactions, fraud alerts, collections.
- Validate account-to-transaction relationships and suspicious activity patterns.

### Healthcare

- Member enrollment, provider, claim, diagnosis, procedure, prior authorization.
- Validate claim timing, provider relationships, and coding rules.

### Retail

- Customer, order, payment, shipment, return, loyalty events.
- Validate order lifecycle and inventory/payment consistency.

## Positioning statement

The platform creates governed enterprise synthetic data ecosystems, not isolated fake rows. It provides scalable, reproducible, and validated data simulation for privacy-safe business and AI use cases.
